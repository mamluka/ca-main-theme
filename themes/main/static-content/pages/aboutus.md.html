About us

How we started

For many years we provide our users the best they can find on the internet, we have dedicated content creators from 2016. We were founded in Germany in 2015 as an independent blog, then the following year we expanded to two writers, our missions always was to bring the most interesting and fresh content to the people of the world.

The Team

Our team now consist of 6 wonderful people, we work from two locations Germany and France, we are also looking for new people to join us check out the careers page

Our vision

We believe in writing high-quality in-depth articles, we believe that knowledge is power and we try to empower the people of the world with our content.
